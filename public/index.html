Walking

Give your dog the experience of joining a pack! Your dog can be a member of my dog walking group.

We meet regularly and under my leadership all dogs learn to coexist and socialize in safe and enriching ways.

The dogs have plenty of fun and recieve the exercise they need to live healthy lives.

Training

All plans are based off of scientific methods using operant and classical conditioning.

Training plans are created specifically for each dog and tailored to fit their needs.

About Me

My name is Jon-Paul. I am a Redwood City native and dog person. I have worked with dogs for many years. I have plenty of experience training, grooming, and caring for dogs of all sizes, but I am more specialized in training larger dog breeds for personal security purposes.

I have four Belgian Malinois which I raised from birth. They have undergone years of training with me and have become very obedient, loyal and loving companions. My pack is my family. I can provide the same level of training for your companions.
